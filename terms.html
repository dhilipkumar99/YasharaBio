2. Description of Services

Yashara provides specialized software platforms for life sciences research and laboratory operations:

  • FluorocellAI is a software application providing AI-assisted cell segmentation and quantification capabilities for fluorescence microscopy image analysis, distributed as a plugin for ImageJ/Fiji and as a standalone application.
